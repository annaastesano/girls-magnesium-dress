Take a look at what the assessment covers

The assessment covers the following aspects of digital transformation through data at news organizations:

  • Strategic direction and data foundations

    Establishing a vision for integrating audience data into decision-making, and ensuring that the organizational culture, skills, data set up and technology support this vision.

  • Recirculation

    Serving relevant and personalised content recommendations to increase the percentage of readers who visit another page on your website after reading the first article.

  • Content Planning

    Using content engagement metrics and reader behavior patterns to inform the content brief, format, channel, and calendar to ultimately drive higher engagement.

  • Reader Experience

    Delivering a great reader experience that improves engagement and propensity to pay by optimising the look, feel, and navigation of the on-site or in-app experience.

  • Subscription Pricing and Promotion Strategy

    Using audience insights to inform subscription pricing and promotional decisions, including price points, discounts, and paywall meter, to improve overall conversion rate and increase revenue per subscriber.

  • Designing for Reader LTV

    Using audience engagement data to understand and track the value of a reader across the reader’s entire relationship with the company (including acquisition, support, and retention) and making business decisions by taking a full view of the reader’s value as opposed to at a given point in time.

  • Revenue Diversification

    Developing and tailoring non-core products and services based on reader interests and behavior to drive additional revenue beyond traditional content and advertising products.

  • Audience-based Advertising

    Using audience data to serve the most appropriate advertisements to readers based on contextual and behavioral data. The objective is to increase the relevance of the advertising to the reader and attract greater digital advertising spend.

  • Advertising Pricing Strategy

    Leveraging audience data to support the advertising sales team in yielding higher per unit revenue, regardless of sales channel, by understanding the relative value of different audience segments to advertisers.

  • Inventory Yield Management

    Using content planning and audience engagement data to forecast future inventory and optimise the mix across direct sales, private marketplaces, and open auctions in order to maximise advertising revenue.
