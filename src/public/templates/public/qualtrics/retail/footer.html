Take a look at what the assessment covers

The assessment covers the following aspects of digital transformation through data at retail organizations:

  • Strategic Direction and Data Foundations

    Establishing a vision for integrating audience data into decision-making, and ensuring that the organizational culture, skills, data setup and technology support this vision.

  • User Interface and Navigation Optimization

    Deliver experiences that delight customers by optimizing the look, feel, and navigation of the on-site or in-app experience.

  • Product Recommendations

    Serve the most relevant product and content recommendations based on a customer’s browsing and transaction history.

  • Promotions and House Ads

    Develop and deliver the retailer’s own advertisements and promotions to drive awareness of promotional offers.

  • Product Assortment and Merchandising

    Analyse customer purchasing behavior to help inform future product catalog and web placement decisions.

  • Customer Loyalty and Lifetime Value (LTV)

    Use data to meet individual customer needs and preferences, improve loyalty, and drive increased LTV.

  • Digital Supplier-Funded Marketing

    Improve the effectiveness of marketing funded by suppliers on a retailer’s site through better campaign execution and creative design.

  • Non-Supplier Marketing

    Utilise customer behavior analytics to monetize advertising inventory sold to non-suppliers through targeted advertising.

  • Offline to Online

    Direct customers toward in-store purchases when they are unlikely to buy online.
