Assessment checklist

The assessment will ask you questions about the following aspects of your organisation’s marketing strategy:

  • How your marketing team is set up, how it works with other teams (such as media buying) and with external partners
  • How tailored messaging is for each of the channels you use
  • What technology platforms you use for marketing purposes (data analysis, content management, insights generation, ad delivery, measurement)
  • How you use data to create campaigns, ads and websites
  • How you monitor the quality and success of your marketing campaigns
  • How often you run marketing experiments or campaign audits
  • Which of your marketing processes are manual, and which are automated
  • The loading speed and usability of your primary owned channels
  • How you build your audiences

Privacy Statement

At Google we care about your privacy.

Emails are never used and not shared with anyone, other than the tool for sending the link to the report.

Raw survey answers are not shared with anyone.
The resulting scores (without emails or personal names) are made available to Googlers to help customers build a learning path, and shared with BCG to allow them to continue their research and benchmarking work.
