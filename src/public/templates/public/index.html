The Digital Maturity Benchmark is a simple way for brands and organisations to figure out which of four levels of maturity they’ve reached, and see where they need to make investments to level up and move closer to Multi-Moment maturity.

Businesses fall into one of the four stages, with only a handful from the original Google and Boston Consulting Group study at the forefront - where marketing is executed dynamically and optimised towards single-customer business outcomes across the full purchase journey.

Your path to multi-moment digital marketing

Nascent
These typically handle marketing on a campaign-by-campaign basis, mainly using external data and direct buys, with only limited links to sales results.
Emerging
They’re characterised by making some use of their own data in automated buying, but are still optimising and testing on a channel-by-channel basis.
Connected
These companies were found to have integrated their data effectively, activating it across channels and linking it to ROI or sales proxies.
Multi-moment
They are seeing efficiency and ROI benefits by successfully connecting with consumers at multiple moments across the purchase journey through personalised content.
