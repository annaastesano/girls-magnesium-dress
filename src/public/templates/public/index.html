Your roadmap to digital marketing maturity

The Digital Maturity Benchmark is a diagnostic tool developed by Google and Boston Consulting Group based on a study to identify the success factors and best practices that drive businesses up the maturity scale.

First, it analyses where your business currently sits on a path to full data-driven marketing and attribution. Then, it generates a personalised report to help you level up and grow.

How it works

Benchmark which stage your organisation is at on our digital maturity scale.

Nascent
Businesses at this stage work on a campaign-by-campaign basis, using external data and direct buys with limited link to sales
Emerging
In these businesses, there is some use of owned data in automated buying, with single-channel optimisation and testing
Connected
By now, data is integrated and activated across channels with a demonstrated link to ROI or sales proxies
Multi-moment
At this stage, businesses have dynamic execution optimised towards a single-customer view across channels
