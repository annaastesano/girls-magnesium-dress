Powerful online diagnostics to track your digital marketing expertise

Digital Maturity Benchmark is a web-based tool from Google that lets you self-diagnose how well your organisation is adapting to the new world of data-driven digital marketing, and helps you transform your business to serve and retain super-empowered customers.

When you or members of your team take the assessment, we’ll follow up with tailored, step-by-step advice on how you can pinpoint the investment and resources needed for digital marketing excellence.
